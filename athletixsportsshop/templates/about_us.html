About Us

AthletiX Sports is Mumbai based sports equipment brand started in year 2020. We provide top quality cricket and fitness equipment at a reasonable rate. All of our products are made in India. Raw material used for manufacturing our equipment are produced in India only. We deliver our products all over India as well as across the world(International courier charges applicable).

Our sole aim is to make high quality sports equipment available for sports enthusiasts at low price point. We do not compromise on quality while deciding price factor of our equipment. Currently we are associated with various upcoming state and district level professional cricketers. With highly positive feedbacks from our customers, we at AthletiX Sports are thriving to achieve new heights with our customer base.
