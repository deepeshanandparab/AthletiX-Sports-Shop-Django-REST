FAQs

Q.1 Who is AthletiX Sports?

AthletiX Sports is Mumbai based sports equipment brand. All our products are manufactured in India. We aim to cater sports enthusiasts by providing high quality sports products at reasonable price point.

Q.2 Are AthletiX Sports brand equipment made in India?

Yes. AthletiX Sports is Indian brand. All the equipment are manufactured in India.

Q.3 What are the delivery charges?

Delivery charges vary depending upon delivery options, locality and product weight. Express delivery charges more than standard delivery which delivers the courier faster.

Q. 4 Do we ship our products internationally?

Yes. We have partnered with trusted logistics company who provides reliable courier, package delivery and express delivery services.

Q. 5 Do we have Order tracking facility?

Yes. Our delivery partners provide a link which redirects to their portal where you can track your order right from placing an order to the delivery.

Q. 6 How many days does it take to deliver the product?

It usually takes 5-7 business days excluding Sundays and public holidays.

Q. 7 Does courier include bill of the purchased item?

Yes. We send proper bill of the product with all the details such as date of purchase, price, seller information as well as other important details.

Q. 8 What is replacement and refund policy?

All the details regarding replacement and refund policy are mentioned in our terms and conditions article. You can review our terms section or simply click on the link to read replacement and refund policy. Link: www.athletixsportsshop.com/replacement_policy

Q. 9 What about order cancellation?

We at AthletiX Sports have strict cancellation policy. All the details regarding cancellation policy are mentioned in our terms and conditions article. You can review our terms section or simply click on the link to read cancellation policy. Link: www.athletixsportsshop.com/cancellation_policy

Q. 10 What if product is out-of-stock?

We usually update our inventory on regular interval. But in case if any product is out of stock, customer can choose an option to get notified as soon as it is available. In case of products with size unavailability, customer can also mention the required size.

Q. 11 What if customer receive a damaged product?

After receiving a product, customer must check the product packaging and product condition at the time of delivery. Regarding Damaged Items in transit, you have 48 hrs to inform us about the same and product must be in the original condition as shipped regarding manufacturing defective items you have 48 hours to inform us about the same with proof in original condition/unused. There will be no replacement if the product if used.

Q. 12 How can a customer contact AthletiX Sports support staff for registering any issue?

Customer can contact on our number mentioned on our website www.athletixsportsshop.com or simply go to FAQs section where customer can post any product related query.
